ABOUT ARTIST

The impressionistic art of Alankar Singh are deeply inspired by dream art and its various aspects. Alankar, who lives in India, is fascinated by the interplay of color, shape and draws upon these elements in various life aspects. Alankar prefers working with pencil, water Color, and acrylics and also have spare time for his hobby. Alankar Singh is also a Engineering student pursuing B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering from University Institute of Engineering & Technology, Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra.


For me art is an expression of an artist which he/she observes in day-to-day life.

- Alankar Singh
